Drinkpocalypse 2022

Awards

Hover over each image for more information about each Drinkpocalypse Award.

The Camel Award

The Camel Award is not for the faint of heart... or faint of bladder. In order to win this award, one must retain water like a camel, and resist the urge to break the seal! The person who takes the least amount of bathroom breaks will also take home this award.

The Howler Monkey Award

In order to win the Howler Monkey Award, you need to be loud and proud and produce the most obscene burp that you can muster! That thing better register on the Richter Scale if you want to win this obnoxious award.

The GOAT award

If you want to win the GOAT Award, you'll need to truly embody the spirit of Drinkpocalypse! What does that mean exactly? We're not really sure either, but we'll figure it out at the end of the day and vote as a group to decide who deserves it most. As a loose guideline you can think of this award as something of a consolation prize for whoever goes all out for Drinkpocalypse.
